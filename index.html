Ten Days, Six Dates

First date, Skirted Heifer for burgers and fries,
Immediate connection and sexual attraction, no lie.

Second date you started reading my mind,
Your dinner was the same as mine.
That's when I showed you my crazy,
Yet, still, you wanted to call me baby!

Third date was a beer and a dainty gin drink,
First kiss, oh my! What did you think?
Your touches gave me tingles, your kisses made me wet,
But wait, no sex just yet!

Fourth date was the Best. Date. Ever.
I wanted it to end, never.
Dressed up at a dive bar drinking Long Island Iced Teas,
Your hands all over me, I wanted time to freeze.

Fifth date was Ted's Montana Grill,
Next, the bar where you gave me a thrill.
It was hard to keep you at bay,
Through your pants I could tell what you'd say.

Date six we went out for sushi,
Then back at your place for a make-out seshie.

Anniversary one meant matching swimsuits and shoes,
Near year two, matching Vegas adventure tattoos.

I survived 9 months without you near,
Now the future without you I fear.
I want to be yours and you to be mine,
But winning you back I know will take time.
